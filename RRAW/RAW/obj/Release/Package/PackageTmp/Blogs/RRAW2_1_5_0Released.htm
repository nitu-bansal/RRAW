RRAW 2.1.5.0 is out now. In this version we have covered some of the last urgent requirements, bug corrections and visual improvements which has increased overall look & feel as well as stability of the RRAW Portal.

  • Master/Navigation Page
    Navigation link "Appr. As Adhoc Air Rates" has been renamed to "Air Freight Rates (ADHOC)" just to streamline display.

  • Dashboard
    If you have tried to click on "Last" button inside the "Recent activity on requests" grid, you must have said that uhh, nothing has happened. You would like to check it once again now.

  • Air Rate Request
    a. You will now be able to attach documents on any of the stage of a rate request (although only if you have rights to!)
    b. Additional selection options "ATA" and "ATD" are available for Forwarder Svc., and XDoc AMS or DTD selection boxes
    c. You will find WD Ship Method selection box with naturally sorted values

  • Air Freight Rates (ADHOC)
    a. "Expiry Date" column has been removed from the grid as adhoc lanes are ment to be expired explicitely by the approver
    b. "HAWB" (HAWB Number) column has been added as the first column in the grid as you are filtering by that one most often
    c. "Request ID" (Air Rate Request ID) column has been added as the second column as you may want to know that from where this lane came in

  • User Interface Improvements
    a. Styling of buttons is improved.
    b. Inside "Air Freight Rates" and "Air Freight Rates (ADHOC)" scrolling and Positioning of action buttons (i.e. Apply Filter, Remove Filter, Export to CSV and Export to Excel) are now fixed and floatable to improve usability.
